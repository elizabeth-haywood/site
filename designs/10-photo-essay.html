A Life Well Lived

A Beautiful Soul

Elizabeth Bowles Haywood graced this world with her presence for 72 remarkable years. Born on October 4, 1953, she possessed a warmth that drew people to her like light.

Her Legacy

She believed the truest measure of a life well-lived was found in the love shared and the hearts touched along the way. By that measure, her life was immeasurably rich.

Elizabeth was blessed with a gentle spirit and an innate capacity for love that would define her remarkable life. From her earliest days, she radiated warmth and joy that touched everyone around her.

II

A Life of Love

Her home was always filled with laughter, the aroma of home-cooked meals, and the warmth of genuine hospitality. Elizabeth believed that the most precious gifts in life were the moments shared with loved ones.

III

Elizabeth faced life's challenges with grace and resilience, teaching those around her the power of perseverance. Her strength was quiet but profound, inspiring all who knew her.
