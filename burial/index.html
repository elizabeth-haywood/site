Please join us to commit her body to the earth on February 7 at Bluestem Conservation Cemetery at 1:00 PM.

What to Expect

  • Walking through a wild and wonderful nature preserve
  • A procession to her resting place
  • Poems and music
  • A green burial ceremony

What to Wear

We will be out in the elements for the full hour, and tromping through a wild and wonderful nature preserve. Please dress comfortably in outdoor clothing and sturdy shoes. Bring an umbrella if needed.

Parking

Park in the lot at the entrance or along the road near the drive. Volunteers will be on hand to help guide you.

The Celebration of Life will be approximately an hour after the burial, so you will have time to change and recharge.
